Pele

Edson Arantes do Nascimento, better known as Pele, is widely considered the greatest footballer of all time. He won three World Cups with Brazil and scored over 1,000 goals in his career.

Diego Maradona

Diego Maradona was an Argentine footballer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. He led Argentina to victory in the 1986 World Cup and is famous for his "Hand of God" goal.

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine forward who has won numerous awards and accolades in his career. He has won the Ballon d'Or award for the best player in the world a record seven times.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese forward who has won numerous awards and titles in his career. He has won the Ballon d'Or award five times and has scored over 700 career goals.
